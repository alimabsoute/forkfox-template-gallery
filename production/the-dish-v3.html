Frequently asked

Is ForkFox a restaurant review app?
No — ForkFox scores individual dishes, not restaurants. Two plates at the same restaurant can earn wildly different scores.
Which cities are live?
San Francisco and Philadelphia, as of April 2026.
How do I join the beta?
Enter your email on the homepage and we'll send a TestFlight invite within 24 hours.
What does a dish score mean?
Five attributes — flavor, texture, value, execution, context — combined into a 0-to-100 score personalized to your taste profile.
Is it free?
Yes. Free during beta.
